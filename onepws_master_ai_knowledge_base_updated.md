# Authoritative Website Timeline - Updated Milestones

Use this section as the final source of truth for year-wise OnePWS/Pyrotech Workspace Solutions milestones in the chatbot training data.

## 2006

In 2006, Pyrotech Workspace Solutions Pvt. Ltd. was founded by Mr. Puneet Talesara, an IIT Guwahati alumnus, with a vision to provide world-class Control Rooms and Control Desk Solutions.

## 2007

In 2007, the company introduced an innovative line of modular control room consoles to the Indian market, redefining efficiency and flexibility. These customizable solutions elevated operational standards and empowered operators with comfort and precision in critical tasks.

## 2009

In 2009, continuing the PWS success story, the company established a cutting-edge manufacturing plant spanning 125,000 sq. ft., strengthening its commitment to excellence and innovation in production.

## 2011

In 2011, the company installed an ultra-modern metal processing plant, using the latest technologies to elevate manufacturing capabilities. This advancement reinforced its dedication to efficiency, precision, and industry-leading innovation.

## 2012

In 2012, the company delivered its first-ever control room outside India. This was a major stepping stone that brought new challenges, learning, opportunities, and access to new markets.

## 2013

In 2013, the company inaugurated a dedicated 10,000 sq. ft. showroom exclusively showcasing cutting-edge control room consoles and interiors. The showroom became a hub for awareness and firsthand experience of OnePWS solutions.

## 2014

In 2014, the company achieved a groundbreaking milestone by becoming the world’s first company to attain Greenguard Gold Certification for consoles. This highlighted its commitment to healthier control room environments and set a new benchmark for the industry.

## 2015

In 2015, designing and delivering control rooms for the Defence & Aerospace sector became a proud milestone for Pyrotech Workspace Solutions. After three months of dedicated effort, the company secured its first order in this sector, fulfilling its aspiration to serve the nation.

## 2016

In 2016, the company hosted Confes’16 – The Control Room Fest, one of the largest events in the Control Room Interiors & Console Industry. The event brought together 180 delegates from 5 countries, 75 companies, and 10 industries, encouraging collaboration across sectors.

## 2017

In 2017, Pyrotech Workspace Solutions achieved 15% year-on-year growth and emerged as India’s market leader in turnkey control room solutions within just nine years. By this point, it had delivered 40,000+ operator consoles and 180+ design-build control rooms across 20+ countries.

## 2018

In 2018, the company became the first and sole company globally to integrate SAP into the console industry. This implementation strengthened data management, process reliability, innovation, and customer confidence.

## 2019

In 2019, the company successfully completed India’s biggest incubation center project: an eight-floor building with a total floor area of 100,000 sq. ft., completed within a record time span of five months.

## 2020

By 2020, over 18 years, the company had grown into a skilled team of 400 professionals with a proven track record of 450+ control rooms delivered, 60,000+ desks installed, presence in 25+ countries, and 100+ appreciation letters. Driven by passion, the company consistently delivered “Beyond the Obvious” results on a global scale.

## 2021

In 2021, the company advanced its journey of excellence with key achievements including the State Safety Award for its commitment to safety and the UCCI Excellence Award for industry leadership. With a turnover of ₹125 Cr, it reinforced its strong market position and consistent growth.

## 2022

In 2022, the company completed the Chandigarh Smart City Control Room and launched its new vertical, Admin Interior. It also delivered advanced facilities such as the Center of Revenue and Research Department and the Rajiv Gandhi Center of Advanced Technology, reflecting its drive for innovation and excellence.

## 2023

In 2023, the company received the Export Excellence Award from FIEO and expanded its footprint by supplying advanced console solutions to IGI Airport, New Delhi. These milestones reflected its commitment to quality and international growth.

## 2024

In 2024, the prestigious alumni award recognized outstanding career achievements and impactful contributions across fields such as business, innovation, leadership, and public service. It celebrated individuals who brought pride to the institution and uplifted lives through their work, including Mr. Puneet Talesara’s distinguished alumni recognition.

## 2025

In 2025, the company launched PWS FLOOR, a high-performance Raised Access Floor (RAF) tile solution engineered for mission-critical environments. Introduced under the trusted legacy of ONEPWS, PWS FLOOR reflects the company’s commitment to quality, innovation, and reliability in advanced infrastructure solutions for control rooms and high-performance workspaces.

## 2026

As of 5 February 2026, all specialized capabilities were unified under one name: OnePWS. The transition represents more than a name change; it reflects a strategic evolution and strengthens the company’s ability to operate as a single, intelligent ecosystem delivering integrated environments at scale.
