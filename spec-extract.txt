OnePWS AI Chatbot — Single-Brand
Master Build Specification
Role
You are a senior full-stack architect and principal engineer. Build a production-grade AI chatbot
web application for OnePWS only.
This chatbot will run on onepws.com and must have knowledge of all business units under
the group, but the product itself is a single-brand application for OnePWS.
Use this document as the single source of truth to generate the complete web app codebase.
1. Product Goal
Build a complete multilingual AI chatbot platform for OnePWS that:
● works on onepws.com
● represents OnePWS as the main brand
● has knowledge of all business units handled by the group
● can answer questions about:
○ control rooms
○ control room consoles
○ auditoriums
○ corporate interiors
○ integrated workspaces
○ raised access flooring / false flooring
○ modular operation theatres
○ healthcare infrastructure / clean room related solutions
● captures leads from visitors through natural multilingual conversations
● qualifies leads intelligently
● identifies which internal business unit should handle the enquiry
● routes leads to the correct department or person
● stores all chat data and lead data in the database
● writes structured lead records into Google Sheets
● sends internal summary emails through Gmail or SMTP
● always CCs the marketing department on internal lead routing emails

2. Product Scope Clarification
This is not a multi-tenant or multi-brand SaaS product for now.
This is a single application for OnePWS only.
However, the chatbot must understand and classify enquiries across all business units
associated with OnePWS.
Important rule
● The chatbot brand identity must always be OnePWS
● The website is onepws.com
● The chatbot may say that certain requirements are handled by the relevant internal team
or business unit
● The chatbot should not behave like three separate bots
● The chatbot should behave like a group-level intelligent project advisor for OnePWS

13. Routing Logic
This is a single-brand OnePWS app, but routing must still identify the correct internal handling
team.
Department routing examples
● control room -> control room team
● consoles -> console team
● auditorium -> project/interiors/auditorium team
● corporate interiors -> interiors team
● raised flooring / false flooring -> flooring team
● modular OT / healthcare infra -> healthcare / OT team
● mixed requirement -> sales coordination / enterprise solutions team
● unknown -> fallback sales email + marketing cc
Person routing behavior
● if a person is explicitly requested, first collect enough lead details
● then check person mapping
● if found, send to that person and CC marketing
● if not found, fallback to mapped department and CC marketing
